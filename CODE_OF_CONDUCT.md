# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

ClawSafe is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for all contributors, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

We value:
- **Respect** — treating others with dignity and professionalism
- **Inclusivity** — welcoming diverse perspectives and experiences
- **Safety** — creating a space where everyone can participate without fear
- **Collaboration** — working together toward shared security and usability goals
- **Integrity** — honesty in communication and code review

---

## Expected Behavior

### As a Contributor

- **Be respectful.** Critique ideas, not people. Disagree constructively.
- **Be helpful.** Answer questions patiently. Mentor newcomers.
- **Be honest.** Disclose conflicts of interest. Report security issues responsibly.
- **Be inclusive.** Welcome people from all backgrounds. Use inclusive language.
- **Be professional.** Keep discussions technical and on-topic.

### As a Maintainer

- **Respond promptly** to issues, PRs, and discussions
- **Provide clear feedback** on code and design
- **Enforce this Code of Conduct** fairly and consistently
- **Credit contributors** in release notes and documentation
- **Make decisions transparently** and be open to appeal

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

We do not tolerate:
- **Harassment** — unwanted advances, insults, or intimidation
- **Discrimination** — based on identity, background, or beliefs
- **Abuse** — hostile or demeaning language or behavior
- **Doxxing** — sharing private information without consent
- **Spam** — repetitive, off-topic, or self-promotional posts
- **Bad faith** — intentional misrepresentation or manipulation

This also includes:
- Sexual or violent content in any form
- Linking to external harassment campaigns
- Deliberate spreading of misinformation
- Attempting to undermine project security or integrity

---

## Reporting & Enforcement

### Reporting a Violation

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Direct message** the maintainer (akafengfeng) if it involves a specific person
2. **Open a private GitHub issue** if the violation is on GitHub
3. **Email** the maintainer for sensitive matters: fengfeng.wf@gmail.com

Please include:
- What happened (with dates/times)
- Who was involved (if safe to share)
- Impact on you or the community
- Any context or prior incidents

**Confidentiality:** We will handle reports respectfully and confidentially. We will not share reporter identity without consent.

### Response & Resolution

- **Acknowledgment:** We will respond within 48 hours
- **Investigation:** We will gather facts and consult with parties involved
- **Action:** Depending on severity, actions may include:
  - Private warning or clarification
  - Public apology or retraction
  - Temporary suspension from participation
  - Permanent removal from the project

- **Appeal:** Individuals can appeal enforcement decisions within 30 days
